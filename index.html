Try this one at home

Caldo Verde

Ingredients

  • An onion
  • 3 clovers of garlic
  • 5 potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper
  • Smoked and sweet paprika
  • Alloro
  • Cabbage
  • A carrot
  • Tabasco

Preparation

I cooked a sliced middle-sized onion with two or three spoons of olive oil, then I added sliced garlic, salt, pepper, a teaspoon of smoked paprika and one of sweet paprika. I then added the potatoes cut into cubes and I left it on the fire for a couple of minutes. After that I added two cups of water and a leaf of alloro. The resulting cream soup will be placed again on the stove with another couple of spoons of olive oil. After that, I add minced kale cabbage. If the soup is too thick you can add some more water. Cook with middle flame for 15 to 20 minutes, until the cabbage softens, then you know it is ready to be served.
